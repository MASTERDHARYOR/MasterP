Call Centre performance Metrics

In today’s customer-driven world, call centers play a critical role in ensuring seamless customer support and service delivery. To enhance operational efficiency and customer satisfaction, businesses need actionable insights derived from data. This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of call center performance, focusing on key metrics such as call handling efficiency, agent performance, customer satisfaction, and call resolution trends. By leveraging interactive visualizations, this report offers a data-driven approach to monitoring call center effectiveness and identifying areas for improvement. Project Objective The goal of this dashboard is to track, analyze, and optimize call center operations by visualizing critical KPIs, helping decision-makers make informed choices that enhance service quality and efficiency. Key Features: ✔ Customer Satisfaction Monitoring – Average satisfaction rating analysis ✔ Call Handling Insights – Answered vs. abandoned calls, call resolution rates ✔ Agent Performance Metrics – Calls answered, response speed, and efficiency rankings ✔ Call Volume Trends – Monthly breakdown for better resource allocation This project showcases my ability to transform raw data into actionable insights using Power BI, highlighting my expertise in data visualization, business intelligence, and performance analytics.
